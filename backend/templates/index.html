Welcome to Thrissur Junction

Thrissur Railway Station (Station Code: TCR) is a major railway junction in Kerala, India.

This station management system provides comprehensive control over:

  • Ticketing Operations - Manage passenger tickets across all classes
  • Goods Management - Track shipments and cargo
  • Store Operations - Monitor retail sales at station stores
  • Train Scheduling - Real-time train tracking and signal control

Please select your role from the sidebar to access the appropriate dashboard.
